This is a potential estimate, not a quote or guaranteed benefit amount. The Making Ends Meet Calculator shows the maximum value an Angeleno could receive if enrolled in every LA City program they qualify for at the maximum benefit level. It is not an application, not a benefits determination, and not a promise of payment. Actual eligibility for each program is set by that program and requires separate enrollment, income verification, and current program availability. Some programs have waitlists, limited capacity, or are currently closed to new applicants. Dollar figures are sourced from LA City departments, the project's fact-checked program inventory, and published cost-of-living data (Zumper, PPIC, LADWP, LAHD, HACLA). Assumptions are editable in making-ends-meet-assumptions.csv. Programs are sourced from making-ends-meet-programs.csv.
